Privacy Policy – Merchzon All In One

Last updated: 9/26/2025

Merchzon All In One is a Chrome extension that helps research Amazon Merch listings and download PNG designs.

Information we do NOT collect

  • No personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • No payment or financial information
  • No browsing history outside Amazon pages used by the user

Local storage only

The extension stores a few settings locally in your browser’s chrome.storage: selected marketplace, niche presets/ZIP codes, and license activation state. This data never leaves your device.

External services

Buttons may open PayPal or LemonSqueezy in a new tab for optional support or license purchase. Those sites are separate services and handle their own data and policies.

Data sharing & selling

We don’t sell, rent, or share your data. No analytics or trackers are included.

Permissions

  • downloads — to save PNG files you choose to download
  • storage — to keep your settings on your device

Questions? Reach out via YouTube or Instagram.
